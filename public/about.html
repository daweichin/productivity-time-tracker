About

This app was built to track how much time I spend doing a specific type of activity. In particular this was motivated by a want to track exactly how much time I was spending learning things passively (eg: watching online courses, youtube videos etc) compared to actively (attemping to build things).

The ultimate goal is to be able to compare the about of time spent doing each type of learning and find a good balance between the two, and also spending time away from learning/working which I denoted by "chilling".
